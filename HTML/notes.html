sub = used for chemical formulas like H2O and closing /sub

sup = used for math equations like x2+3x+4 and closing /sup

div = you can use it to divide up the page into different sections, div element is a generic container for whatever other elements you might want to put inside and closing /div

ol = ordered list and puts number infront of items and closing /ol

ul = unordered list and puts bullet points in front of items and closing /ul

li = list item and can only be used insid ol or ul and can be considered void tag with no closing or closing /li
